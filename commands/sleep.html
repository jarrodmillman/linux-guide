sleep¶

Name¶

sleep – delay for a specified amount of time

Synopsis¶

sleep {number} [suffix...]

Description¶

Pause for NUMBER seconds. SUFFIX may be `s’ for seconds (the default), `m’ for minutes, `h’ for hours or `d’ for days.
