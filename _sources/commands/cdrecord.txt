cdrecord
========

Name
----

cdrecord -- record audio or data Compact Discs from a master

Synopsis
--------

**cdrecord** [option] {dev=device} [track options...] {trackn...}

Description
-----------

Cdrecord is used to record data or audio Compact Discs on an Orange
Book CD-Recorder.
